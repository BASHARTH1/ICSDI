Venue

The Art Hotel, Kingdom of Bahrain

ICSDI 2026 takes place at The Art Hotel in the Kingdom of Bahrain, in hybrid mode — with full virtual access for participants worldwide.

A Gateway to the Gulf

Bahrain sits at the heart of the Gulf region, well connected to the wider GCC and known for its heritage sites, beaches and vibrant conference scene. The Art Hotel offers a comfortable setting for two days of research, dialogue and collaboration.
