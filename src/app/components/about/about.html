About the Conference

A Global Platform for Sustainable Innovation

The 2nd International Conference on Sustainable Development and Innovation (ICSDI 2026) brings researchers, practitioners, policymakers and innovators together in the Kingdom of Bahrain.

Hosted by Gulf University, ICSDI 2026 is structured around the 17 United Nations Sustainable Development Goals. It aims to foster interdisciplinary dialogue, research collaboration and innovative practices that address the most pressing challenges of sustainable development globally.

Held in a hybrid format at The Art Hotel, the conference welcomes both in-person and virtual participation, connecting a worldwide community of scholars and industry leaders. All accepted papers are published by Springer Nature and indexed in SCOPUS.

  • Peer-reviewed original research
  • Interdisciplinary SDG-aligned tracks
  • Distinguished international keynote speakers
  • Networking with academia, industry & government
